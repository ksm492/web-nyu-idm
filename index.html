The Story of My Name

Why Kiana?

My parents chose my first name for three reasons:

  • It is from the Kayanian Dysnasty in Persia
  • In Farsi, it means "Fire, Earth, Wind, and Water"
  • In Fillipino, it means "Mountain to Sea" (My sister's name, Kaila, is the inverse of my name; it means "Sea to Mountain")

Why Sassani?

My parents chose my middle name for two reasons:

  • It is from the Sassanian Dysnasty in Persia
  • It rhymes with "Dasani"

Why Menguita?
